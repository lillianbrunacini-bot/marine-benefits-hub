BAH and BAS are tax-free
Base pay is taxable. BAH and BAS are not. This significantly increases your effective compensation vs. the base pay number alone.

MyCAA for spouses
Spouses of O-1 to O-3, W-1 to W-2, and E-1 to E-5 qualify for up to $4,000. Apply at MyCAA.com before your spouse starts a program.
BAH is yours to keep
If your rent is less than your BAH rate, you keep the difference tax-free. BAH covers median local housing costs — not a ceiling on your budget.

OHA for OCONUS
Stationed overseas? BAH is replaced by Overseas Housing Allowance (OHA), which covers actual rent up to a local cap plus utilities. Ask the AI tab for your specific location.
Document everything now
A VA disability rating depends on a documented service connection. Record every injury, exposure, and medical event on active duty. Don't wait until separation.
